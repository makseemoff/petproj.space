Privacy Policy for Blocky QR

Effective Date: March 9, 2026
Last Updated: March 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Aleksei Maksimov, the developer of Blocky QR ("we", "us", "our"), handles data when you use Blocky QR (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. What We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information on our own servers.

The App processes and stores certain data locally on your device to provide its features, including:

  • QR content you enter (for example, text, URLs, and Wi-Fi fields).
  • Images you choose from your device gallery for QR logo customization.
  • Generated QR previews, local history entries, and logo file copies used by the App.
  • Export preferences (for example, selected export size).

This local data is not sent to our servers.

2. Permissions We Use

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Photo/Media Library access (read): to let you choose an image from your gallery.
  • Photo/Media Library access (add/save): to save exported PNG images to your gallery.
  • Internet and network state access: to load and display ads.

If you deny a permission, related features may not work.

3. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to show rewarded ads.

AdMob is a third-party service. Google may process certain data (such as device information, advertising identifiers, IP address, ad interactions, and diagnostics) to provide, measure, and improve ads, according to its policies.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not share your locally stored QR content with our servers.

Data may be processed by Google as part of AdMob ad delivery and related ad operations.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Local App data remains on your device until you remove it.

You can delete local data by:

  • Removing App data from your device settings; or
  • Uninstalling the App.

You can also revoke app permissions in your device settings at any time.

6. Children

The App is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed to children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If we make changes, we will post the updated version and update the "Last Updated" date.
